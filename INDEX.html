He gained fame with the TV series "Pavitra Rishta" 📺 and made a successful transition to film 🎬 with notable roles in "M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story" 🏏 and "Kedarnath" 🏔️. Renowned for his versatility and depth 🎭, Rajput quickly became a beloved figure in the entertainment industry 🌟. His untimely death on June 14, 2020 😞 shocked the nation and sparked important discussions on mental health 🧠. Despite his tragic end, his legacy 🌟 and celebrated body of work 🎥 continue to inspire ✨😌.
